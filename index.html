Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +

Simply click on the 'Get Yours Made' button on the pricing card and fill out our contact form with your details!

Can I request a specific design, or add my initials? +

Yes! Each pair is crafted to match your preferences. You can request specific colors, designs, and even add initials or logos for a personal touch. Just let us know what you have in mind!

What if my shoes don't fit perfectly? +

We aim for a perfect fit, but if something's off, we'll work with you to make necessary adjustments and ensure your satisfaction with our fit correction process.

How do I maintain my handmade shoes? +

Use a damp cloth to wipe them clean and apply leather conditioner periodically for longevity.

How do I pay for my order? +

Once you submit your order, we'll contact you directly with payment details and confirm your custom shoe design.

Do you offer worldwide delivery? +

Yes! We ship worldwide. Delivery times vary based on location, and shipping rates apply. Contact us for more details on international shipping options.

Do you accept bulk or wholesale orders? +

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Contact us to discuss wholesale options.

Can I get my shoes faster if I need them urgently? +

We offer an express service for urgent orders at an additional cost. Let us know your deadline, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

About Us

Ilisabawn is a proudly Nigerian brand specializing in bespoke shoes that offer the perfect blend of fit, durability, and refined style. Since our founding in 2018, our footwear has stood as a testament to impeccable craftsmanship and premium quality.

At Ilisabawn, we don't just make shoes, we craft experiences. Every pair is designed to embody elegance, comfort, and timeless sophistication. Step into bespoke luxury and discover the difference with Ilisabawn.
